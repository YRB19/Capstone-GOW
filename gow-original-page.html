The Legend Begins

In 2005, Santa Monica Studio introduced the world to Kratos, the Ghost of Sparta, in a game that would revolutionize action-adventure gaming and spawn one of PlayStation's most iconic franchises.

Haunted by the murder of his family and cursed by the gods, Kratos serves Olympus for ten years. When Athena offers to remove the nightmares that torment him in exchange for one final task, Kratos must kill Ares, the God of War himself.

This groundbreaking PS2 title combined brutal combat, epic boss battles, intricate puzzles, and Greek mythology into an unforgettable experience that set new standards for the genre.

The original God of War is where Kratos' legendary journey of vengeance began - a tale of rage, redemption, and the price of defying the gods.

The original game received universal acclaim for its innovative combat system, epic scale, and emotional storytelling, establishing Kratos as one of gaming's most iconic characters.
