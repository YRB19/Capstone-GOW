A Personal Journey

Set between the events of God of War and God of War II, Ghost of Sparta explores Kratos' quest to discover the truth about his mysterious past and his family.

Haunted by visions of his mortal past, Kratos embarks on a journey to the lost city of Atlantis and the volcanic domain of Thera to uncover the origins of his nightmares.

This PSP exclusive delivers a console-quality experience in the palm of your hands, with stunning graphics and the series' signature brutal combat.

Discover the secrets that shaped Kratos into the Ghost of Sparta and witness the events that drove him deeper into darkness.
