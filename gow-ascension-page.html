Vengeance Begins

God of War: Ascension is the prequel to the entire God of War series, taking place before the original game and revealing the origin of Kratos' journey of vengeance.

Betrayed by the gods and losing everything he held dear, Kratos seeks to break free from the chains of the Furies who torment him for breaking his blood oath to Ares, the God of War.

This chapter explores Kratos at his most human moment, before he became the Ghost of Sparta, showing his transformation from man to god-killer.

Experience brutal combat, innovative time manipulation mechanics, and the first multiplayer mode in the God of War franchise.

For the first time in the series, God of War: Ascension introduces competitive and cooperative multiplayer modes where players align with one of four gods and battle for supremacy.

Two teams compete to earn favor by defeating enemies and capturing objectives.

Match of Champions

Free-for-all deathmatch where only the strongest warrior survives.
