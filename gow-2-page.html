Betrayal and Redemption

After claiming the throne of the God of War, Kratos is betrayed by Zeus and stripped of his godly powers. Cast down to the mortal realm and on the brink of death, Kratos is rescued by the Titan Gaia.

With the Titans as his allies, Kratos must journey to the Island of Creation and find the Sisters of Fate, who can send him back in time to prevent his betrayal and have his revenge on Zeus.

Experience the most epic God of War adventure yet, pushing the PS2 hardware to its absolute limits with stunning visuals and massive scale battles.

This is the tale of a god's fall from grace and his relentless pursuit of vengeance against the King of Olympus.
