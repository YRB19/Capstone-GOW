Before the Fall

Set 10 years before the original God of War, Chains of Olympus reveals Kratos at the height of his service to the gods of Olympus as their champion warrior.

When the sun god Helios is kidnapped and the dream god Morpheus unleashes nightmares upon the world, Kratos must journey to the Underworld itself to prevent catastrophe.

This PSP adventure delivers full God of War experience with console-quality graphics and epic gameplay on a handheld device.

Witness Kratos before his ultimate betrayal, when he still believed the gods would honor their promises.
