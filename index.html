The Epic Saga

Starting with the release of God of War in 2005, Santa Monica Studio has created an unforgettable gaming legacy spanning multiple platforms and generations.

The latest chapter sends Kratos and Atreus on a mythic journey for answers before Ragnarök arrives. Together, father and son must put everything on the line as they journey to each of the Nine Realms.

Throughout stunning mythological landscapes, they'll face fearsome enemies from Norse gods to wild beasts as they prepare for the showdown of their lives. All the while, Asgardian forces assemble...

Meet the Characters

Legendary Warriors

Kratos

The Ghost of Sparta, wielding the Leviathan Axe and Blades of Chaos. A father fighting to protect his son and prepare him for the world.

Atreus

Son of Kratos, skilled archer discovering his true identity and destiny in the Norse realms.

Thor

The Norse God of Thunder, wielding Mjölnir. A formidable adversary standing in their path.

Odin

The All-Father of Asgard, a cunning and powerful ruler with mysterious motives.
